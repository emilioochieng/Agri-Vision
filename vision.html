About AgroVision

Farming has always run on data. We just make it visible.

What AgriTech is. AgriTech is the application of data analytics, GIS, IoT, AI and cloud technology to agriculture — turning field conditions into information farmers can act on in time to matter.

Why we exist. Smallholder farmers produce most of Africa's food but have the least access to the data that governs their industry: weather, soil, and price. AgroVision closes that gap.

Our story. This platform began as a portfolio project exploring how data engineering, Power BI and modern web tools could solve a real agricultural problem — and grew into a working model for what farm-level data infrastructure could look like at scale.
